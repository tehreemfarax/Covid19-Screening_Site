Coroa Virus disease (Covid-19) is a special disease caused by a new virus.

The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as cough. fever, and in more severe cases. difficulty breathing. How it spreads?

Coroa Virus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it,then touch their eyes,nose or mouth.
